# llama.cpp daily summary — 2026-04-14

A moderately active day with 12 commits to upstream/master. The headline additions are a new Vulkan Flash Attention DP4A shader for quantized KV cache, a dedicated DeepSeek V3.2 chat parser, and CUDA ARGSORT correctness fixes for graph mode. WebUI also received two improvements around MCP diagnostics and chat title generation.

## CUDA/GPU

- **CUDA**: `DeviceSegmentedSort` now falls back to the slower `DeviceSegmentedRadixSort` when running inside a CUDA graph (immediate mode was incorrectly used in capturable contexts, causing correctness issues) — #21718 `9f5e1ed`

## Vulkan
